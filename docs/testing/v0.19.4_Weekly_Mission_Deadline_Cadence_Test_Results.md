# v0.19.4 Weekly Mission Deadline Cadence Test Results

Date: 2026-07-19

## Summary

Verified the weekly Thursday deadline cadence end-to-end after merging `origin/main`
(`v0.19.5`, PR #48) into the `fix/v0.19.4-mission-task-checklist` branch: accepting a mission
now stores `deadlineAt` as the earliest end-of-Thursday (23:59:59.999 server-local) at least 4
inclusive calendar days from acceptance, with `graceEndsAt` exactly `gracePeriodHours` later.
All weekday branches (Monday keeps this Thursday; Tuesday–Thursday roll to next week;
Friday–Sunday get the coming Thursday) and midnight/end-of-day boundaries are unit-tested
timezone-portably; a rule-based regression scenario asserts the invariants on a real acceptance
against the live local stack (run on a Sunday, the stored deadline landed on the coming
Thursday — 5 inclusive days). The v0.19.4 checklist scenario was updated for `v0.19.5`'s new
submission-readiness gate (full evidence URLs + four attempt journals via
`submitRegressionSubmission`). All gates pass.
