# Spiral Engineering Method (SEM)

TalentOS follows the Spiral Engineering Method.

Participants complete the full engineering lifecycle from Day 1.

The lifecycle remains constant while complexity increases.

## Lifecycle

1. Discover
2. Analyze
3. Specify
4. Design
5. Build
6. Test
7. Deploy
8. Present
9. Reflect
10. Production Readiness Review

## Core Principle

Do everything every week.

Increase complexity every week.

Do not separate theory from practice.
