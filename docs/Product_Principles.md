# Product Principles

1. AI augments learning; it does not replace thinking.
2. Learning happens through building.
3. Every mission ends with a deployed artifact.
4. Production readiness is more important than coding speed.
5. Every participant builds a public portfolio.
6. Every assignment mirrors a real-world engineering process.
7. AI usage must be transparent.
8. Every feature should encourage independent problem solving.
9. The platform must be white-label.
10. The platform must support multi-company SaaS deployment.
