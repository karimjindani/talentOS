# Product Model

TalentOS is a multi-tenant white-label SaaS platform.

## Target Market

- Companies
- Universities
- Bootcamps
- Training Institutes
- Internal Corporate Academies

## Delivery Models

### Open Source

Organizations deploy TalentOS themselves.

### Hosted SaaS

Organizations subscribe to a managed version provided by Paysys Labs.

## White Label Features

Organizations can customize:

- Branding
- Logos
- Certificates
- Email Templates
- Knowledge Bases
- Programs
- Missions
