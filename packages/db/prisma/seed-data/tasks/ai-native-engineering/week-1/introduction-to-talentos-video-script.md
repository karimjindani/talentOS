# Introduction to TalentOS - Three-Minute Video Outline

Status: final YouTube URL pending. The video will be produced in NotebookLM outside TalentOS.

## Opening

- Welcome the applicant to TalentOS.
- Explain that TalentOS is a structured four-week engineering journey built around evidence and reflection.

## Weekly Learning

- Introduce weekly tasks and their Markdown and video resources.
- Explain that required tasks prepare the applicant for the week's mission.

## Missions

- Explain that each accepted applicant receives a weekly mission assignment.
- Describe missions as practical engineering work using the Spiral Engineering Method.

## Engineering Journal

- Show that journal entries capture work, challenges, solutions, learning, AI usage, confidence, time, and evidence.
- Explain that the journal is linked to the current assignment attempt.

## Submission And Review

- Explain repository, deployment, and Loom evidence.
- Explain the readiness checklist: required tasks, at least four current-attempt journal entries, and publicly accessible URLs.
- Explain that staff may accept, request revision, or ask the applicant to repeat the week on a new attempt.

## Closing

- Emphasize that evidence proves what was built and reflection shows how the applicant thinks and grows.
